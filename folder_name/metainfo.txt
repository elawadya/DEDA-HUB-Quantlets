Name of Quantlet: Credit Risk Prediction in P2P Lending

Description: "Empirical analysis of credit-risk prediction in peer-to-peer lending using the Bondora public loan dataset. The project constructs alternative default labels, compares logistic regression, LightGBM, TabPFN-3, and Bondora's published Probability of Default score, evaluates discrimination and calibration across prediction horizons, and applies split-conformal prediction to quantify uncertainty under country and time-based distribution shift. Visualizations cover country-level default patterns, label sensitivity, probability calibration, model-horizon performance, vintage effects, and conformal coverage."
